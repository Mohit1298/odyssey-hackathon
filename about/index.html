Welcome to E-Mission! Our goal is to help the government of Brampton and its citizens by creating a globally accessible website for tracking high usage and release of fossil fuels and electricity. Through this, we wish for the ministry of environment and interested individuals to be able to cross check this map with upcoming and completed environmental projects as we continue to improve the state of the environment. Its super easy to use! The colours represent the intensity of usage and emissions. This grants awareness for the user in their quest to fix hotspot areas one at a time.
